Frequently asked questions

How fast will I receive my development?

On average, most requests are completed in three days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer depending on the scope and technical requirements.

How does onboarding work?

Subscribe to a plan and we'll quickly add you to your very own project dashboard. This process usually takes about an hour to complete from the time you subscribe. Once you accept the invite, you're ready to start submitting requests.

Who are the developers?

Boost Agency is a specialized agency with a handpicked team of expert developers. You'll work directly with our team through the entirety of your experience, ensuring consistent quality and communication.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can make?

Once subscribed, you're able to add as many development requests to your queue as you'd like, and they will be delivered one by one in the order received.
